The Plastic Problem

Humanity has a problem. Pollution, we’re addicted to it. One of the largest contributors to this addiction is our love of plastic. Why? Because we are lazy and love convenience. It’s in everything, from packaging to construction to medical equipment. But when we’re done with it, where does this manmade material go? This presentation will attempt to answer the below questions using the datasets outlined:

Where does it come from - https://ourworldindata.org/where-does-plastic-accumulate

What are the impacts - https://ourworldindata.org/ecological-impacts-of-marine-plastic-debris

What’s being done - https://www.coastalcleanupdata.org/reports
